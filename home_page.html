Welcome to behind Bach's lenses

My name is Bach, and I am currently a third-year majoring in Computer Science and Finance. Aside from spending the majority of my time at school working, something that I enjoy doing a lot is photography. Photography helps me reset my calm after working long hours with numbers and codes.

My preferred type of work is storytelling. My photos usually serve as a means to convey a story, typically about relationships. I love working with different tools, from DSLRs to travel cameras or even my phone. My current set of tools includes a Canon 80D with a 50mm f1.8, a Sony Alpha a6400 paired with a 35-150mm lens, and a Fujifilm z5 travel camera.

My favorite settings for a shot really vary, from a darker outdoor place such as a bar counter, to a good day of sunlight outdoors, somewhere like a park. For me, the ability to let my creative side go free and shoot quality pictures is fulfilling. After a good day of shooting, I will export my work and take a good look at them, seeing what type of story I would like to write. These could be from my own experiences, a story that I heard, or just a figment of my imagination.

All of my stories are published in the Instagram page above. Have a tour around there if you are feeling romantic!
